: In recent years, the industry's interest in improving its production efficiency with AI algorithms has grown rapidly. Especially advancement in computer vision seem promising for visual quality inspection. However. the proper classification or detection of defects in manufactured parts based on images or recordings requires large amounts of annotated data, ideally containing every possible occurring defect of the manufactured part. Since some defects only appear rarely in production, sufficient data collection takes a lot of time and may lead to a waste of resources. In this work we introduce a configurable, reusable, and scalable 3D rendering pipeline based on a digital reality concept for generating highly realistic annotated image data.
